About us
.....more than just a flower shop!

Props – a name you’ll want to remember.
Creative flair, personalized service, and fabulous flowers.
Our inspired designers can’t wait to create something fresh for your next occasion,
your business environment, or anytime you just feel like having flowers around. Planted in 1998,
Props has grown into the florist of choice for their discerning customer base.
You’ll find an oasis of style and colour,
brimming with ideas and inspirations at our shop located at The Hydrostone Market 5531 Young Street.
Owner and lead designer Susan MacIntosh with 25 years’ of proficiency in custom floral design ensures the “Props Team” focusses on meeting the varied requirements,
and tastes, of a diverse client base.

Props is so much more than just a flower shop, it’s a love affair with flowers!
